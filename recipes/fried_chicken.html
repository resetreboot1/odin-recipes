Fried Chicken

Description

Fried chicken is a flavorful dish made by coating chicken pieces in seasoned flour or batter and cooking them until golden brown and crispy. It's typically deep-fried or air-fried, offering a juicy inside with a crunchy outer layer, and is enjoyed worldwide for its satisfying texture and rich taste.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • 1 Cup buttermilk
  • 2 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Black pepper
  • Paprika (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp Garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp Onion powder
  • 3/4 cup All purpose flower
  • 1/4 cup Cornstarch

Steps

  1. Soak chicken in butter milk for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Add flour + all the spices + cornstarch.
  3. Coat the chicken in the flour mixture.
  4. Preheat air fryer (180°C) 3 mins.
  5. Air fry the chicken at 180°C for 25-20 mins
  6. Let it cool down and serve
