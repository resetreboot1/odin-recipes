Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made with a round, flat base of dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various ingredients like vegetables, meat, or herbs. Baked until crispy and melted, it’s loved worldwide for its delicious flavor and endless customization.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups All purpose flower
  • 2.25 tsp Yeast
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • Olive oil
  • 1 cup Water
  • Pizza sauce
  • cheese (any)
  • Desired toppings
  • 1 Cup warm water (Not Hot!)

Steps

  1. Activate yeast by mixing sugar, warm water and yeast
  2. While waiting for the yeast to activate add flour in a large bowl and add salt,oil
  3. After activation pour the yeast mixture in the flour misture and mix well until you can pick it up
  4. Pick up the dough and knead it well
  5. Transfer the dough in a oiled container and cover it with cling wrap
  6. Wait for it to rise.Meanwhile chop up toppings
  7. After it doubled in size take it out and put it on a lightly floured surface and shape it how you want
  8. Add the pizza sauce and chesse and toppings
  9. Cook in air fryer at 200°C for 8-12 minutes
  10. Cool down and serve
