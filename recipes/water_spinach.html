Water Spinach

Description

Water spinach is a tender leafy green, perfect for air frying. Lightly seasoned and cooked in the air fryer, it becomes crisp-tender with a deliciously roasted flavor. It's a healthy, easy side dish packed with nutrients and great with garlic or chili.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups Water Spinach
  • 1 tsp oil
  • salt (to taste)
  • 1 clove garlic

Steps

  1. Wash the spinach
  2. Dry the spinach
  3. Add dried spinach, garlic, oil and salt in a bowl and mix
  4. Preheat the air fryer to 160°C for 3 mins
  5. Cook spinach mix in the airfryer at 160°C for 8 mins
  6. Cool down and serve
