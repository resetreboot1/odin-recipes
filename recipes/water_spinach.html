Water_spinach

Description

Water spinach is a tender leafy green, perfect for air frying. Lightly seasoned and cooked in the air fryer, it becomes crisp-tender with a deliciously roasted flavor. It's a healthy, easy side dish packed with nutrients and great with garlic or chili.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups Water Spinach
  • 1 tsp oil
  • salt (to taste)
  • 1 clove garlic
